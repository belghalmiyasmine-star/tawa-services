1. Create src/features/admin/actions/commission-queries.ts ("use server"):
- getCommissionOverviewAction(): checks ADMIN role. Returns:
  - commissionRate: 0.12 (12% hardcoded)
  - totalRevenue: sum of payment.amount where status in [RELEASED, REFUNDED] — total platform revenue
  - totalCommission: sum of payment.commission where status in [RELEASED]
  - totalPayouts: sum of payment.providerEarning where status = RELEASED
  - pendingPayouts: sum of payment.providerEarning where status = HELD
  - withdrawalsPending: count of WithdrawalRequest where status = "PENDING"
  - withdrawalsProcessed: sum of WithdrawalRequest.amount where status = "PAID"
  - monthlyCommission: [{ month: "YYYY-MM", commission: number, revenue: number }] last 6 months (in-memory groupBy from payments)

- getProviderPayoutsAction(page=1, pageSize=20): checks ADMIN role. Returns paginated list of providers with their financial summary:
  - For each provider with at least 1 payment: provider displayName, userId, totalEarnings (sum providerEarning RELEASED), totalCommission (sum commission RELEASED), pendingAmount (sum providerEarning HELD), withdrawalsTotal (sum WithdrawalRequest amount PAID), lastPayoutDate.
  - Ordered by totalEarnings DESC.
  - Total count for pagination.
